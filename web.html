In shadowed corners, whispers rise,
Ivory keys like moonlit skies,
Each touch a breath, a tender sigh,
Notes cascade like stars that fly.
Fingers dance on polished plains,
Echoing dreams, embracing pains,
Melodies weave a tapestry,
Binding hearts in harmony.
The wooden frame, a soul's embrace,
Holds secrets of time and space,
With every chord, a world unfurls,
A symphony of silken pearls.
In silence found between each phrase,
Resides the pulse of endless days,
The piano sings, a timeless friend,
A lullaby that knows no end.
